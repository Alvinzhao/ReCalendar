Restoring Maniana Backups

Maniana backup is done by sending an attachment file to your Gmail account.

Для восстановления резервной копии:

  1. In the Gmail app, open the message with the backup attachment.
  2. Click on the Download button near the attachment.
  3. Maniana will show you a menu with import options.
The backup/restore was tested with the Gmail app. It may or may not work with other email clients.
